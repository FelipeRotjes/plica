About the project


Plica


Plica is a Miami-based art project that promotes and showcase the work from local visual artists throught books, original design and installation.

Creative director


Carlos Fabián Medina


A recent Miami transplant, Carlos Fabián is a multidisciplinary artist from Caracas, Venezuela, that works with performing and visual arts.


Based in Miami since 2019, Fabián enjoys developing ideas and projects that connects to local artists, stories and communities; through film, theater, images and books. Recent Knight Foundation's Knight New Work 2020 winner.

About the artist


Mikhael Scarr


Scarr uses drawings, wordplay, and site-specific installations to despict macro and microevents. His satirical works address such topics as politics in the post-truth era and the artist´s role and identity in society.


Scarr responds with puns, laughter, and ridicule to the institutionalization of terror and violence, greed, alienation.


The drawings, incisive and witty, seek to identify rupture moments, displacement, and dislocation within the psychosocial maelstrom.

Mikhael Scarr


Scarr's notebook interventions question meaning and identify, reality and consciousness in the age of bureaucratic administration, anonymous labor, elite domination, and mass hallucination.


Mikhael Scarr was born in 1974 in Paris, France. He lives and works in Miami, where settled in 2013.
